Nashville, officially Nashville-Davidson, is the metropolitan government of Nashville and Davidson County. This form of government consolidation (city and county governing entity) is rare in the United States.

Nashville was named after Francis Nash, a general of the Revolutionary War whom assisted George Washington. Nashville has a rich history in many domains - from music (known as the "Music City") to war (the first Confederate state capital to fall).

To this day, Nashville continues to be a hub for icon enthusiasts and musical talent.
