Memphis, Home of the Blues

  • Year Founded: 1819
  • Population: 628,127 ( 2021 estimation )
  • Region of Tennessee: Southwestern (West Tennessee)
  • Classification: 100% urban
  • Median Household Income: $44,317
    • Source: city-data.com

Memphis, like other Tennessee cities, has rich musical heritage. The city is known as the "Home of the Blues" because of its development and popularization of blues music.

Further, Memphis was home of the infamous Elvis Presley and Sun Studio - the recording studio for Johnny Cash, BB King, and others. Thus, many consider Memphis as the birthplace of rock 'n' roll.

Memphis boasts a strong BBQ culture and a history ripe with civil rights movements; where music, history, and BBQ come together.
