Gatlinburg, Gateway to the Great Smoky Mountains

  • Year Founded: early 1800's
  • Population: 3,726 ( 2021 estimation )
  • Region of Tennessee: Eastern (East Tennessee)
  • Classification: 100% rural
  • Median Household Income: $46,422
    • Source: city-data.com


In the heart of the Great Smoky Mountains, Gatlinburg serves as a popular base for those exploring the national park. The town is nestled in a picturesque setting of forests and Appalachia mountain views.


Gatlinburg is located just 30 miles outside of Knoxville, thus serving more as a tourist hub than a living populace. Though, for those that seek it, isolation is just a hiking trail away.
